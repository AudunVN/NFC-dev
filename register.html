Comments or Additional Information

To make this convention financially possible we need a minimum of 600 attendees by the 30th of December 2016. If this threshold is not reached the convention will be cancelled. Upon cancellation the ticket fee and hotel rooms booked through NordicFuzzCon will be refunded. However, NordicFuzzCon will not refund any other expenses (e.g., travel costs). All other bookings are at the risk of the attendee.

You will be informed if the threshold was not reached in said timeframe.

In the case of a cancellation by an attendee of either a room booking or a convention ticket (including Sponsor and Super-Sponsor add-ons), NordicFuzzCon will not be able to offer any refunds. Instead we offer the possibility to transfer any bookings or tickets, should the attendee wish to pass on either to someone else.
